Exhibitions & Events

A chronicle of Alena's artistic journey — from the first exhibition in 2013 to recent international shows and collaborations.

  • September 2025 · Prague · SANTE

    Amazing evening, amazing place, amazing people!

    Thanks a million to @sante_praha for hosting my art collection "Confessions should be better planned", to @wine.you.prague for supporting my idea in all the ways, to @trambitskaya_photo for being my dearest content maker!!! And everyone who attended and keeps attending!

  • May 2025 · Prague · SANTE

    A presentation of the art collection "Confessions Should Be Better Planned"

    On the 23rd of May, a cozy place SANTE in Prague is welcoming the audience for a special event: A presentation of the art collection "Confessions Should Be Better Planned" by Alena Martsyanava.

  • February 2025 · Warsaw · ALAR art-beauty shop

    Confessions should be better planned 1.0

    On February 16, 2025, the art-beauty shop ALAR in Warsaw launched the first expo of Alena's collection "Confessions should be better planned". This time only 8 works from this very load of paintings were exhibited to the audience.

  • January 2025 · Italy

    "Contro la Violenza sulle Donne" expo

    So proud to have had a chance of participating in the event raising awareness of the domestic violence towards women in Italy. My artwork "oops I spilt the paint" organically fitted into the collection of works and drew a lot of attention.

  • January 2025 · Wroclaw · Students Artworks Foundation

    Auction

    Alena's artwork in an unusual technique – Grattage – took part in the auction organized by Students Artworks Foundation and was sold to the local art admirer.

  • December 2024 · Minsk · Beetlejuice cafe

    Interpretation of moments: boys vs girls

    Another join-forces-expo with a famous rock musician from Minsk — Eugene Buzouski. The expo took place in the iconic Beetlejuice cafe in Minsk in December 2019 and lasted over 2 months.

  • December 2024 · Minsk

    The "Dream on" Expo

    The first time Alena introduced a new young talent — Anna Trusevich from Minsk. In October 2019 the artworks were presented.

  • December 2024 · Minsk

    The "35" Expo

    Dedicated to Alena's 35th anniversary. The Stary Olsa band performed. The expo contained exactly 35 artworks and was available the whole April 2018.

  • November 2024 · Minsk

    Сhaotic still-life

    Took place in autumn 2014 while Alena was expecting her baby. About 25 new works of various techniques: from oil painting to sketches and Grattage.

  • November 2024 · Minsk

    First Expo

    First Alena's Expo happened back in 2013 with 30 artworks. A local rock band performed at the opening. It lasted 30 days and attracted more than a thousand visitors.
